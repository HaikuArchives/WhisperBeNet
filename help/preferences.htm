The preferences

 

GENERAL

You can decide which view/window you want to see when Whisper starts.
 

AUDIO

Using the codec, you'll compress the sound. Therefore, you'll use less bandwidth. You can go further and use modem quality. This will compress even more the sound, using a modem bandwidth, but degrading the sound very much.

 
 

VIDEO

You can tell Whisper to start capturing video when the application starts. You can also choose to limit the video sampling rate and specify the delay between each frame (in msec). It won't change anything on your side, it's only to reduce the bandwidth.
 

NETWORK

In the "Network" panel you'll be invited to enter the IP of your IP Server (if you know one) and port to connect to (it should always be 3030). Also, you can set timeout (5 is the default) for your connection to the IP Server. You can also set Whisper to go online on startup.
 

IDENTITY

That's where you enter your personal informations. This is how the Java IP server will know you and how other users will find you through that server.
